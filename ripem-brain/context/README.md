# Context

**Purpose**: Captures the evolution of RiPeM — original brainstorm summaries, key design decisions, core assumptions, and open questions.

**For Whom**: Agents understanding "why" decisions were made and what still needs validation.

## Overview

This section is the institutional memory of RiPeM. Before making a significant decision, check here to understand what has already been decided, what was considered and rejected, and what questions remain open.

## Key Sections

- [conversation_summary.md](./conversation_summary.md): Original brainstorm and concept summary
- [design_decisions.md](./design_decisions.md): Key design decisions and rationale
- [assumptions.md](./assumptions.md): Core assumptions and hypotheses
- [open_questions.md](./open_questions.md): Open questions for future research

## Quick Reference

- **Always check design_decisions.md** before making architectural or product decisions
- **Always check open_questions.md** to see what needs validation
- **Document new decisions** in design_decisions.md as they are made
