# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Phase 1: Seed Power Users (Pre-Launch)

**Goal**: Build a base of highly engaged builders before public launch.

**Tactics**:
1. Identify 20–50 "power builder" accounts in niche Facebook Groups (e.g., Honda Civic mods, Subaru WRX builds, Miata owners)
2. Offer exclusive beta access and white-glove onboarding
3. Capture their builds on RipEm; use their content as social proof
4. Seed content on TikTok and Instagram using their real builds

**Target communities**:
- Facebook Groups: platform-specific mod communities (100K+ member groups)
- Reddit: r/projectcar, r/Justrolledintotheshop, platform-specific subs
- YouTube: Collaborate with small-to-mid car build channels
- Instagram: #projectcar, #cargram, platform hashtags

## Phase 2: Launch (App Store Launch)

**Goal**: Convert community interest to app downloads.

**Tactics**:
1. Coordinate influencer builds going live simultaneously
2. App Store Optimization (ASO) for "car build app", "project car tracker"
3. Product Hunt launch
4. Press outreach to automotive media (Motor Trend, Jalopnik, The Drive)

## Phase 3: Viral Growth Loop Activation

**Goal**: Let the watermark do the work.

**How it works**:
1. Users publish builds → content carries RipEm watermark
2. Watermark includes app name + subtle CTA
3. Viewers download app after seeing builds on TikTok/Instagram
4. Network effect: more builds → more discovery → more users

## Key Metrics to Track

- CAC by channel
- Viral coefficient (K-factor) from watermark shares
- Day 7 / Day 30 retention by acquisition channel
- Premium conversion rate
