# Financial Projections

> This document contains the 3-year financial model for RiPeM. Projections are estimates and should be updated as real data becomes available.

## Key Assumptions

| Assumption | Value | Source |
|------------|-------|--------|
| TAM (North America) | 15M enthusiasts | Market research |
| Year 1 MAU target | 25,000–50,000 | Internal estimate |
| Year 3 MAU target | 200,000+ | Internal estimate |
| Premium conversion rate | 5–10% of MAU | Industry benchmark |
| Premium price (monthly) | $4.99–9.99 | To be validated |
| Ad RPM (parts search) | $2–5 CPM | Automotive niche benchmark |
| Organic acquisition % | 60–70% | Via watermark viral loop |
| Avg. downloads/month by month 12 | ~2,500 | Internal estimate |

## Year 1 Model

| Metric | Month 6 | Month 12 |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Registered users | 5,000 | 25,000 |
| Paying users (7% conversion) | 350 | 1,750 |
| MRR (premium ~$7 blended) | ~$2,500 | ~$12,500 |
| Server / infrastructure costs | ~$5,000 | ~$8,000 |
| Team costs | ~$30,000 | ~$45,000 |
| Gross margin | -$32,500 | -$40,500 |

> Year 1 operates at a loss; growth and engagement validation is the primary goal.

## Year 2–3 Outlook

- Scale to 200K+ users with organic + paid growth
- Increase premium conversion to 10%+ via improved retention
- Introduce affiliate revenue ($2–5 per conversion)
- **Year 3 projected revenue**: $300K–$500K/month

## Blended ARPU Model

| Segment | % of Users | ARPU/Month | Contribution |
|---------|-----------|------------|-------------|
| Free users | 85–95% | $1.50–3 (ads + affiliate) | Base |
| Premium users | 5–15% | ~$7 | Upside |
| **Blended average** | — | **$2–4/month** | — |

## Gross Margin

- **Target gross margin**: 60–70% (healthy SaaS margins)
- **Primary cost driver**: AI API ($0.01–0.05/user/month) and infrastructure ($0.50–1.50/user/month)
- AI costs are declining over time; margins improve at scale
