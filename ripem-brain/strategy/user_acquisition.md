# User Acquisition

## Acquisition Strategy Overview

RiPeM's acquisition strategy is **organic-first** with targeted seeding. The watermark loop handles scale; human seeding handles the cold start.

## Phase 1: Seeding (Pre-Launch)

**Goal**: Recruit 20–50 power builders who will become the foundation of the community.

**Channels**:
1. **Niche Facebook Groups**: Platform-specific mod groups (Honda, Subaru, Miata, etc.)
   - Target groups with 50K–500K members
   - Find active builders (post frequently, get lots of engagement)
   - DM with personal invite to exclusive beta

2. **Reddit**: r/projectcar, r/Justrolledintotheshop, platform-specific subreddits
   - Look for build threads; reach out to OPs

3. **YouTube Comments**: Find build channels with engaged audiences
   - Target: 10K–100K subscribers (more accessible than mega-influencers)

**Key principle**: Hand-select people who will create compelling content, not just use the app.

## Phase 2: App Store Launch

**Channels**:
- App Store Optimization (ASO)
  - Target keywords: "car build tracker", "project car app", "car maintenance log"
- Product Hunt launch
- Press outreach: The Drive, Jalopnik, Motor Trend, Petrolicious
- Reddit launch post (r/projectcar, r/cars)

## Phase 3: Paid Amplification (After PMF)

**Channels** (only after product-market fit is validated):
- Meta Ads: Target car enthusiast interest segments
- TikTok Ads: Car culture audiences
- Sponsoring car YouTubers

## CAC Targets

| Channel | Target CAC | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Organic (watermark) | ~$0 | Core loop |
| Influencer seeding | <$5/user | Based on comp + audience size |
| Paid social (Phase 3) | <$10/user | To be validated |
