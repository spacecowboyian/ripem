# MVP Timeline

> Dates to be populated once development resources and start date are confirmed.

## Full Build Timeline (From Business Plan)

The following estimates assume a small founding team working on the complete MVP across all three phases:

| Phase | Duration | Team | Deliverables |
|-------|----------|------|--------------|
| Design & Planning | 2 weeks | 1 PM, 1 Designer | Wireframes, user flows, tech spec |
| Backend MVP | 4 weeks | 2 Backend engineers | API, database, auth, voice transcription |
| Frontend MVP | 4 weeks | 2 Frontend engineers | iOS app, voice UI, chat interface |
| AI Integration | 2 weeks | 1 ML/AI engineer | Claude/OpenAI integration, context system |
| Testing & Polish | 2 weeks | 1 QA + team | Bug fixes, performance optimization |
| **Total** | **~10–12 weeks** | **5–6 people** | **Production-ready MVP** |
