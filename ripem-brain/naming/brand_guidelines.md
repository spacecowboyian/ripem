# Brand Guidelines

## Name

- **App name**: RipEm
- **Written form**: RipEm (capital R, capital E, no space)
- **Never write**: RIPEM, ripem, Ripem, Rip-Em

## Voice & Tone

### Personality
RipEm sounds like a knowledgeable friend who's been building cars for years. Not a corporate tool. Not a generic SaaS product.

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Authentic | Speaks like a real enthusiast, not a tech company |
| Confident | Knows cars; speaks with authority |
| Honest | Admits uncertainty; never overpromises |
| Energetic | Matches the energy of car culture |
| Direct | Gets to the point; no corporate fluff |

### Do Say
- "Rip your next session"
- "Log it while it's fresh"
- "Your build, documented"
- "10,000 Civic owners did this"

### Don't Say
- "Leverage synergies"
- "Seamless experience"
- "Unlock your potential"
- Anything that sounds like it came from a SaaS marketing deck
