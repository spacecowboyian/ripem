# Naming Exploration

## Brainstorm History

The naming process explored several directions before landing on RiPeM.

### Direction 1: RPM-Inspired
Names that riff on RPM as a metaphor for progress and car culture:
- RPM.log
- RevLog
- RiPeM ✅ (chosen)
- RevTrack
- Redline

### Direction 2: Build/Project Focused
Names that emphasize the build tracking function:
- BuildLog
- ProjectCar
- GarageLog
- WrenchLog
- ShopLog

**Rejected because**: Too generic, descriptive, and forgettable. No personality.

### Direction 3: Community/Social Focused
Names that emphasize the community sharing aspect:
- CarReel
- BuildFeed
- GarageGram
- CarStory

**Rejected because**: Too close to existing social platforms, unclear core value.

### Direction 4: AI/Intelligence Focused
- MechAI
- CarBrain
- DriveGPT
- AutoIQ

**Rejected because**: Doesn't capture the building/documenting core loop.

## Evaluation Criteria

Each name was evaluated against:
1. **Memorability**: Easy to remember and spell
2. **Car culture authenticity**: Sounds like it belongs in car culture
3. **Social shareability**: Easy to say in a sentence
4. **Domain/trademark availability**: Checked for conflicts
5. **App Store discoverability**: Good for ASO

## Shortlist

| Name | Memorable | Car-Native | Social | Notes |
|------|-----------|-----------|--------|-------|
| RiPeM | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | Chosen |
| Redline | ✅ | ✅ | ⚠️ | Already used widely |
| RevLog | ⚠️ | ✅ | ⚠️ | Too descriptive |
| BuildLog | ❌ | ⚠️ | ❌ | Generic |

## Final Decision

**RiPeM** — See [ripem_rationale.md](./ripem_rationale.md) for full justification.
