# Naming

**Purpose**: Documents the app name decision, naming rationale, exploration history, and brand guidelines.

**For Whom**: Agents writing marketing copy, defining brand voice, or reviewing naming decisions.

## Overview

The app is named **RiPeM**. The name was chosen for its TikTok-style two-syllable rhythm, its RPM automotive energy, and its natural fit in car culture conversations.
