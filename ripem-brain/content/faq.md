# FAQ

## General

**Q: What is RiPeM?**
A: RiPeM is an AI-powered app for car enthusiasts to log, track, and share their vehicle build projects. Think of it as a voice-first build journal + AI mechanic + community sharing platform.

**Q: Who is RiPeM for?**
A: Anyone actively working on a car project — from a weekend oil change to a full engine swap or restoration. If you spend money on your car and want to remember what you've done, RiPeM is for you.

**Q: Is RiPeM free?**
A: Yes. The core app is free. The free tier includes voice logging, build timeline, and AI assistant. Ads appear only in the parts search section. A premium subscription removes ads and unlocks social posting and analytics.

---

## Logging

**Q: How does voice logging work?**
A: Tap "Log Session," speak naturally about what you worked on, and stop. RiPeM transcribes your voice and automatically extracts parts, costs, and time. You review and save.

**Q: Does voice logging work without internet?**
A: Yes. Voice capture is recorded offline. Transcription and AI extraction happen when you're back online.

**Q: Can I edit what the AI extracted?**
A: Absolutely. Every extracted field is editable before you save.

---

## AI Assistant

**Q: What can the AI answer?**
A: Questions about your specific car and build — compatibility questions, "what should I do next" advice, part recommendations, troubleshooting. The AI knows your car, your history, and community data.

**Q: Is it just ChatGPT?**
A: No. RiPeM's AI is specialized for automotive context. It knows your specific car, your installed parts, and what other builders with the same car have done. Generic AI gives generic answers. RiPeM gives answers about your build.

**Q: What if the AI isn't sure?**
A: Every AI recommendation includes a confidence score (High / Medium / Low). If the AI isn't confident, it says so.

---

## Publishing & Community

**Q: What is the watermark?**
A: When you publish a build, RiPeM automatically adds a watermark to your photos/videos. This is how RiPeM grows — viewers of your content see the watermark and can find the app. On free tier, watermarks cannot be removed.

**Q: Can I remove the watermark?**
A: Premium subscribers can disable watermarks on published content.

**Q: Where does my published build appear?**
A: Published builds appear in the RiPeM community discovery feed. You can also share a link to any platform (TikTok, Instagram, etc.).

---

## Privacy & Data

**Q: Who owns my build data?**
A: You do. You can export your entire build history at any time.

**Q: Does RiPeM sell my data?**
A: No. We do not sell or share user data with third-party advertisers.

**Q: What data does RiPeM collect?**
A: Vehicle information, build session logs, parts data, and usage analytics. We collect only what's needed to provide the service.
