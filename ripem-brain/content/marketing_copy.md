# Marketing Copy

## App Store Copy

### App Name
RipEm — Car Build Tracker

### Subtitle (30 chars)
Log builds. AI assistant.

### Description (Short)
RipEm is the app built for car enthusiasts who actually build. Log your sessions with voice, get AI-powered answers about your specific car, and share your build with a community that gets it.

### Description (Full)
**Document your build. Ask your AI. Share the journey.**

RipEm is the first app purpose-built for car project builders. Whether you're restoring a classic, building a track car, or modifying your daily, RipEm keeps it all organized.

**Voice-First Logging**
Finish a session, tap record, and talk. RipEm transcribes your voice and automatically extracts parts, costs, and time. No typing. No spreadsheet. Just talk.

**AI That Knows Your Car**
Ask anything: "What should I upgrade next?" "Is this part compatible?" "What have 1,000 other Civic owners done?" RipEm's AI knows your build history and gives honest, specific answers — not generic internet advice.

**Share Your Build**
Publish your build with one tap. RipEm auto-generates a compelling update from your logged sessions. Auto-watermarked. Share to TikTok, Instagram, or the RipEm community feed.

**Built for Enthusiasts**
No dark patterns. No fake urgency. No ads in your build log or AI chat. Just a tool that makes documenting your project effortless.
