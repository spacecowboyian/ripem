# Business Terms

This glossary covers business and finance terms used in RiPeM documentation.

---

## A

**ARR** — Annual Recurring Revenue. Total recurring revenue normalized to a one-year period.

**ASO** — App Store Optimization. The process of optimizing an app's listing to increase visibility in App Store search results.

## C

**CAC** — Customer Acquisition Cost. The average cost to acquire one new user. RiPeM targets low CAC via organic (watermark) acquisition.

**Churn** — The rate at which users stop using the app. Monthly churn = users lost / total users in that month.

**Cohort analysis** — Analysis of a group of users who joined in the same period, tracked over time. Used to measure retention.

## F

**Freemium** — A business model where a product is available for free with optional paid upgrades. RiPeM's model.

## G

**GTM** — Go-to-Market. The plan for launching a product and acquiring early users.

## K

**K-factor** — The viral coefficient. Measures how many new users each existing user brings in. K > 1 = viral growth.

**KPI** — Key Performance Indicator. A measurable value that demonstrates how well an objective is being achieved.

## L

**LTV** — Lifetime Value. The total revenue a single user is expected to generate over their lifetime with the product.

**LTV:CAC ratio** — The ratio of lifetime value to acquisition cost. A ratio of 3:1 or higher is considered healthy.

## M

**MAU** — Monthly Active Users. The number of unique users who engage with the app in a given month.

**MRR** — Monthly Recurring Revenue. Total recurring revenue in a given month.

**Monetization** — The strategy for generating revenue from the product.

## P

**Payback period** — The time it takes for a user's revenue to cover the cost of acquiring them.

**PMF** — Product-Market Fit. The stage where a product satisfies a strong market demand. Indicated by high retention and organic word-of-mouth.

**Premium** — In RiPeM: the paid subscription tier. Generally: a higher-value tier of a freemium product.

## R

**Retention** — The percentage of users who continue using the app after a given period (Day 7, Day 30, etc.). The most important metric for consumer apps.

## T

**TAM** — Total Addressable Market. The total market demand for a product if 100% market share were achieved.

**SAM** — Serviceable Addressable Market. The portion of TAM that can realistically be targeted.

**SOM** — Serviceable Obtainable Market. The portion of SAM that can realistically be captured in a given period.
