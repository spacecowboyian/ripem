# Glossary

This glossary defines terms used throughout the ripem brain. When a term has a specific meaning in this project that differs from its everyday meaning, that project-specific meaning takes precedence.

---

## A

**ADR** — Architecture Decision Record. A document that captures a significant architectural decision, its context, and its consequences. See [`/brain/06-decisions/adr/`](../06-decisions/adr/).

**Agent** — An automated tool (such as GitHub Copilot or another AI coding assistant) that reads and modifies code in this repository. Agents must follow the rules in [`/brain/09-agents/`](../09-agents/).

## B

**Brain** — The knowledge base for ripem, located at `/brain`. It is the single source of truth for all product, architectural, and operational information.

**Business rule** — A constraint or behaviour defined by the product domain, not by technical implementation. Business rules are documented in `02-domains/[domain]/business-rules.md`.

## D

**Domain** — A bounded area of business functionality (e.g., authentication, payments). Each domain has its own subdirectory under `02-domains/`.

## I

**Ingestion** — The process of converting raw notes, meeting notes, or transcripts into structured brain documents. Raw material lives in `11-ingestion/`.

## P

**Platform** — Either `ios` or `web`. iOS is the primary platform; web is secondary.

## R

**ripem** — The product being built in this repository. *(Definition to be expanded as the product evolves.)*

---

> **Note:** This glossary is a living document. Add new terms as they are introduced.
